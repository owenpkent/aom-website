Angels of Mercy

A critique of the medical model of disability wrapped up in a horror film

Directed by Andrew Balcof and written by Owen Kent

A quadriplegic hacker faces off with a cult of eugenicists who invade his home.

Thank you to everyone who helped us make this film

When we set out to make this film in 2015, we had no idea how we would be able to do it. It is thanks to you, our generous and patient patrons, that this dream is nearing realization. We intend for this film to explore what it means to have a disability in the modern era and provide a fresh look at the horror genre. As we near completion, we ask that you sign up below to stay updated on the progress of the film.
